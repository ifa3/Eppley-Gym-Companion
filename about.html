About this project

Eppley Gym Companion helps UMD students avoid wasted trips to the gym by combining estimated crowd levels, simple hours, and local weather.

Problem

Students arrive at Eppley without knowing how crowded it is or whether their one-time UID entry has already been used.

Stakeholders

UMD students, RecWell staff, and administrators benefit from clearer information about capacity and access rules.

Data sources

Supabase stores sample crowd and UID data, OpenCage geocodes Eppley, and Open-Meteo provides basic College Park weather.
